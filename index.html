Hidden Hazards Lurk: Is Your Home a Safe Haven?

Everyday items can pose risks to curious children. SafeHaven helps you identify and eliminate these dangers, creating a safe and nurturing environment.

  • Unsecured furniture that can tip over
  • Sharp corners and edges at toddler height
  • Electrical outlets within reach
  • Accessible cleaning supplies and medications
  • Dangling blind cords

Did you know that thousands of children are injured in home accidents each year? SafeHaven can help you prevent accidents before they happen.
